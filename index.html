Interests

My academic interests are centered around both the fundamental and advanced areas of computer science. I am particularly interested in subjects such as Operating Systems, Data Structures, Algorithms, and Object-Oriented Programming, as they form the essential foundation for building efficient and reliable software systems.

I also have a strong interest in emerging fields like Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and Image Processing, which enable intelligent systems to analyze and learn from data. Alongside this, I am passionate about Software Engineering, Curriculum Development, and Academic Advising, as I believe in guiding students and contributing to quality education. I enjoy working with programming languages and tools such as Java, C++, C#, C, and MATLAB, and I am deeply engaged in research, E-Learning, and innovative teaching approaches that connect theoretical concepts with practical applications.
